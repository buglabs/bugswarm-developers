Swarmlight

Swarmlight is one of the first applications built on the BUGswarm platform and makes use of a BUGbase, x10 outlets, and BUGswarm Tools to flip the Chistmas lights in the Bug Labs office on and off by simply using the controls below from anywhere in the world.
