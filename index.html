Welcome to BUGswarm

A brand new M2M platform that gives your devices a voice on the internet. See what you can build with real-time communication between computers.

About BUGswarm

Create Groups

Group resources together by adding them to swarms. Once a resource is part of a swarm, it can communicate with other resources in that swarm.

Control Access

Restrict the way resources access a swarm, giving some permission produce and consume data while limiting others to only producing or consuming.

Share with Others

Invite other users' resources into your swarms, and join their swarms. If you create something useful, share it!
