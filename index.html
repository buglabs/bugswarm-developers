Welcome to BUGswarm

A brand new M2M platform that gives your devices a voice on the internet. See what you can build with real-time communication between computers.

About BUGswarm

Presence

BUGswarm is one of the few cloud messeging platforms that supports real-time presence of physical devices and web applications.

Get social

Join your resources in another user's swarm or invite somebody else's resources to join yours. With BUGswarm, users can easily share each other's data and information.

What's included

Configure groups of devices and web applications through the use of a RESTful API and watch them communicate with each other through the use of HTTP streaming.
