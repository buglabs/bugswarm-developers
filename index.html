Welcome to BUGswarm

BUGswarm teaches devices how to speak the language you know. Organize both hardware and software in swarms, and listen in on the conversation. It includes javascript and BUGbaselibraries to get up and running as quickly as possible.

About BUGswarm Features, introduction and support

Presence

Our messaging platform is one of the few that support real-time presence notifications that work much like a chat room.

Standards

We expose a RESTful web service that supports real-time communications and streaming.

What's included

Native libraries exist for javascript and the BUG series of hardware, with planned support for arduino, android, and whatever else the community challenges us to come up with.
