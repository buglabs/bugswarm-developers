Welcome to BUGswarm

BUGswarm is a way to acquire data from and control embedded devices using JavaScript or plain old HTTP.

About BUGswarm

Create Groups

Group resources together by adding them to swarms. Once a resource is part of a swarm, it can communicate with other resources in that swarm.

Control Access

Control the way resources access a swarm. Grant a resource permission to produce data, consume data, or do both.

Share with Others

Invite other users' resources into your swarms, and join their swarms. If you create something useful, share it!
